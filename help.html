How To Play

Basic instructions on how to play the game for new players

Step 1

Opening The Game

When you first open the game up, you will be greeted with the main menu. Click the play button and the game starts

Step 2

The Game

Now you have started the game, you can see many things. At the top, is the amount of credits, sparks and houses you have. On the left is the current map and on the right is the building menu and next turn button

Step 3

Placing Your First Power Station

The first thing you want to do is place down a power station. When you click on a power station in the building menu (on the right hand side of the screen), you can place the power station down. Some map tiles might be greyed out if you can't place the power station there, for example water tiles. Once you click on a tile, the amount of options is greatly reduced as all spaces need to be adjacent to each other. Once you select the appropriate amount of spaces, you can click the confirm button. if you make a mistake, you can press the cancel button and try again

Step 4

Ending Your Turn

After you have placed down enough power stations, you can end your turn by pressing the end turn button on the bottom right. This will bring up a pop-up, which will give you a review of the year so far. Every year, you will recieved 5 credits to maintain your power stations, but if you generate more sparks than 10, you can sell any excess for one credit per spark. Watch out though, as if you don't generate 10 sparks, you will lose a house for every spark less than 10. You will also have to pay to maintain all of your power stations and some might break as well, which will need to be fixed next turn

Step 5

Ending The Game

After 5 years have gone by, the game will end. You will receive a score based on how many people are still living on the island, how many credits you have, how much damage you have done to the environment and more. Based on this score you will also find out if you were re-elected. You have now finished your first game of Energy Island Project!

Menu

Settings

Press this button to bring up the settings menu. Here you can make the game go into full screen or change the volume


Start Game

Press this button to start the game. Once you start a game you can't change your settings so make sure they are ok

Help

Press this button and the help page will load up. There is a help button in-game too, so you can easily find this page if you are stuck or need help

Game Info

Game Info

At the top of the screen you can see the amount of money, sparks and houses that you have. Money is used to buy and maintain power stations. It is also used to repair them when they break. Your sparks show how much power has been generated this turn. You can sell excess power, but if you don't have enough, some people will move out. Finally, if the amount of people living on your island becomes 0, you lose

Map

In the middle of the screen there is the map. The map is an 8 by 6 grid where you can place power stations. The different tiles are represented by different colours on the grid. By clicking on a tile, you will be shown some information about the tile, such as its name. If its a power station, there will be extra infomation about the power station too


Building Menu and Next Turn Button

The building menu is located at the right of the screen. Here you can see all the power stations you can place. Clicking on one of the buttons will allow you to place a power station down. Underneath this is the next turn button, which will end your current year and start the next one

FAQ

How do I quit once in a game

Press the escape button to open the pause menu. From here you can quit and view the help page

The game isn't working

Sadly, the game isn't perfect. If you encounter a bug, please report it using the infomation in the page footer and you will have to restart your game. Sorry for any inconvenience

Can I play on mobile?

Sadly, the game isn't supported on mobile, so play at your own risk. The game could crash and might not function, so it isn't recommended
