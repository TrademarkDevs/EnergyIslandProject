The Game Where You Need To Power An Island

Energy Island Project is a game that balances strategy and management in an attempt to power an island. You must build a power grid and decide if you will sacrifice the environment to put more money in your pocket or whether you will protect the environment and maybe lose your place in government.

Welcome to The Energy Island Project. This game was developed by William Talwar and the idea was created by Mr Bain. This game is designed to simulate what it is like to manage an island's energy grid and lets you choose how you will produce electricity. It allows you to make your own choices and you can see if you will be re-elected after 5 years.

In Energy Island Project, you place down power stations to power an island. Each power station has different pros and cons and you will need to decide what you think the best strategy is. The game is short and is intended to be played many times, with the idea to make the best power production island. After 5 years, you will recieve a score based on the amount of houses left, how much money you have and how much you have damaged the environment.

Throughout the game, you will receive chance cards that will dramatically change the game. These could vary from getting more energy from your power stations to more extreme situations such as your power stations getting destroyed or adverse climate change issues causing probems for the island. This makes every game different and keeps players on edge, as they need to adapt and work out how to live with the effects of the chance cards.
