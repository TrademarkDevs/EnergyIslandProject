The Game Where You Need To Power An Island

Energy Island Project is a game that balances strategy and managment in order to try and power an island. You need to try and power an island while also not destroying the environment, by deciding if you would sacrafice the environment to put more money in your pocket or if you will protect the environment and maybe lose your place in government.
